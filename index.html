about us

Nengi Benson Integrated Services is company that offer various services ranging from industrial cleaning, interior decoration and fumigation for homes and offices. We are committed to ensuring that the needs of our clients are met with distinct quality and excellent delivery.

We have the experience and expertise to deliver top-quality results, ensuring that your dream home or office becomes a reality. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you with your project.
We handle every aspect of your project, ensuring that you receive a seamless and stress-free experience.

Nengi Benson Integrated Services has been on a fast path of growth and development with highly skilled and technically capable manpower with strong belief and passion in the philosophy of delivering projects safely, on schedule and within budget maintaining international standards using leading technology / equipments.

As part of our service, we will:

  • Conduct weekly site inspections and attend meetings as and when requested.
  • Tailor a cleaning plan according to your needs and create a cleaning schedule accordingly
